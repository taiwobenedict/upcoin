Step 1: Preparation:

  • Make sure you have at least 0.015 ETH in your wallet to cover the gas fee. Or about $20 worth of ETH

Step 2: Access Supcoin Website:

  • Open your web browser and go to Supcoin.co. The website should display the Supcoin platform.

Step 3: Click the "Connect" Button:

  • Locate and click the "Connect" button on the website's interface. You'll typically find this button at the top-right corner of the screen.

Step 4: Choose "Wallet Connect":

  • After clicking "Connect," a pop-up box will appear with various wallet connection options. Look for and select "Wallet Connect" among the options.

Step 5: Scan the QR Code:

  • A QR code will be displayed on the website's screen. To connect your Trust Wallet, open the Trust Wallet app on your mobile phone.
  • Within the Trust Wallet app, look for the "Wallet Connect" feature. It's usually found in the settings or options menu.
  • Use the Trust Wallet app to scan the QR code displayed on the website's screen. This action establishes a secure connection between your Trust Wallet and the website.

Step 6: Connection Confirmation:

  • Once you've successfully scanned the QR code, you'll notice that you are now connected. This connection status is often indicated at the top-right corner of the website's interface

Step 7: Select Purchase Option:

  • You will want to switch to BNB by clicking on “Buy with BNB”
  • Once connected to the BNB Smart Chain, click the "Buy Sup with ETH" button.

Step 8: Initiate Purchase:

  • Click "Buy Sup with USDT."
  • Ensure you have sufficient USDT in your wallet to cover the purchase.
  • If you have enough USDT and ETH for the gas fee, proceed; otherwise, you'll receive a warning.

Step 9: Choose Purchase Amount:

  • You can choose to spend the maximum amount of USDT by clicking "Max."

Step 10: Confirm Purchase:

  • Once you've made your selections, click "Buy Now."
  • On your trust wallet app, you should get a notification asking if you want to “Confirm Your Payment”
  • Click "Approve" to complete the purchase on Trust Wallet app
  • Note: You may get a warning message “if you are using your trust wallet through a Binance Smart Chain and that it is a high risk, be careful and make sure you trust this site” that is normal. Just make sure you are on Supcoin.co

Step 11: Completion:

  • Congratulations! You've successfully purchased SUP tokens using USDT on the ERC20 network.

Step 12: Further Assistance:

  • If you encounter any issues or have concerns, refer to the troubleshooting page on the platform.
  • You can also join the Telegram or Discord communities to get additional assistance from moderators.

That's it! You've completed the process of buying SUP tokens using USDT on the ERC20 network. Please be cautious and do your research when investing in cryptocurrencies.
